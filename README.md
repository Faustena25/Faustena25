## What I build

### 🔬 LinguaLens
**Multilingual Sentiment Analyzer for Indian Social Media**

Code-switching is everywhere in Indian text — Hinglish, Tanglish, mid-sentence language shifts that break standard NLP tools. LinguaLens handles it.

- Model: `XLM-RoBERTa` fine-tuned on code-switched corpora
- Stack: `Python · Flask · SQLite · Chart.js · Bootstrap`
- Focus: Sentiment + language detection across mixed-script social media posts

> *Live project — MSc Project I submission, 2025–26*

---

### 🎙️ VoicePulse
**Longitudinal Voice-Based Mental Wellness System**

Not a mood tracker. Not a questionnaire. VoicePulse sits quietly on top of your existing voice assistant, learns what *you* normally sound like, and notices when something has shifted — over days, not moments.

- Signals: Acoustic (pitch · energy · tempo) + Linguistic (word pattern drift)
- Architecture: On-device · local · zero data exfiltration
- Intelligence: Personalised nudges grounded in *your* emotional history — not population averages

> *What makes it different: it watches the story of your emotional life, not a single frame of it.*
